# Forge Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 15, 2026**
**Effective Date: June 15, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

Forge ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Forge application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our Service.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@forge.app.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Registration Information:**
- Name, email address, password, phone number
- Company name, industry, team size
- Billing address and payment information (processed by Stripe)
- Workspace configuration and preferences

**User-Generated Content:**
- Documents, files, and messages you create or upload
- Collaboration history and workspace activity
- Team member information and invitations
- Profile information and preferences

**Communication Data:**
- Support tickets and communications with our team
- Feedback, surveys, and feature requests
- Email correspondence and inquiry details

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Information:**
- Access logs (IP address, browser type, operating system)
- Pages visited, features used, duration of visits
- Clicks, interactions, and feature engagement
- Error reports and performance metrics

**Device Information:**
- Device identifiers and hardware specifications
- Browser information and cookies
- Mobile device information (if applicable)

**Location Information:**
- Approximate location based on IP address
- Time zone information
- Geographic location of login attempts (for security)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery**: Providing, maintaining, and improving the Service
- **Authentication**: Verifying your identity and securing your account
- **Billing**: Processing payments and managing subscription information
- **Communication**: Sending transactional emails (confirmations, receipts, updates)
- **Customer Support**: Responding to inquiries and resolving issues
- **Compliance**: Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service
- **Analytics**: Understanding usage patterns and improving Service performance
- **Marketing**: Sending promotional communications (with your consent)
- **Security**: Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and protecting against attacks
- **Research**: Conducting research to improve our product and services

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Service Providers

We share your information with third-party service providers who assist us:
- **Stripe**: Payment processing and subscription management
- **SendGrid/AWS SES**: Transactional email delivery
- **AWS**: Cloud infrastructure and data hosting
- **Monitoring services**: Error tracking and performance monitoring
- **Analytics providers**: Aggregate usage analysis

All service providers are contractually bound to use your information only for providing services to us and are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal process, court orders, or government requests
- Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements
- Protect the security or integrity of the Service
- Protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of Forge, users, or the public

### 4.3 Business Transfers

If Forge is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will provide notice before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

### 4.4 Public Information

Any information you choose to make public in your workspace (shared documents, public profiles) may be accessed and used by other users or external parties.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after deletion
- **Payment Information**: Retained for 7 years for legal and tax compliance
- **Backup Data**: Retained per our backup retention policy (30-day automatic backups)
- **Logs**: Retained for 90 days for security purposes
- **Deleted Content**: May be recoverable for 30 days before permanent deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through account settings or by contacting privacy@forge.app.

## 6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access with principle of least privilege
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication support for enhanced security
- **Monitoring**: CloudWatch monitoring and CloudTrail audit logging
- **Firewalls**: AWS WAF and security group configurations
- **Regular Audits**: Periodic security reviews and penetration testing
- **Incident Response**: Documented procedures for security breaches

Despite our security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 7. GDPR Compliance (for EU Users)

If you are in the EU, you have the following rights under GDPR:

### 7.1 Data Subject Rights

- **Right of Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we process your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Oppose processing for marketing or profiling
- **Right to Lodge Complaints**: File complaints with your supervisory authority

### 7.2 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:
- **Contract**: Necessary to perform our service agreement
- **Consent**: You explicitly consent to processing
- **Legal Obligation**: Required by law (tax, regulatory)
- **Legitimate Interests**: Our business interests balanced against your rights

### 7.3 Data Protection Officer

For GDPR inquiries, contact: dpo@forge.app

### 7.4 Standard Contractual Clauses

International data transfers are governed by EU Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy determinations.

## 8. CCPA Compliance (for California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under CCPA:

- **Right to Know**: What personal information is collected, used, and shared
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of collected information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information
- **Right to Correct**: Correct inaccurate personal information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: No discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

To submit a request, contact: privacy@forge.app with "CCPA REQUEST" in the subject line.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for:

- **Authentication**: Maintaining your logged-in session
- **Preferences**: Remembering your settings and choices
- **Analytics**: Understanding how users interact with our Service
- **Security**: Preventing fraud and detecting attacks

**Cookie Types:**
- **Essential**: Required for Service function (cannot be disabled)
- **Analytics**: Help us understand usage (can be disabled)
- **Marketing**: Track conversions and user behavior (can be disabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit Service functionality.

## 10. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. When you click external links, you leave our Service and are subject to their privacy policies.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Forge is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.
